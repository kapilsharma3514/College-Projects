Important tips for CAT Exam

  • If you are doing your Graduation than start preparing from second year of graduation.

  • If you completed Graduation and ready to give the Cat in next coming November, Prepare ar least 10- 14 hours daily and seriously.

  • Minimum 10 hours study required before the 6 months of Cat exams.

  • Minimum 10,000 Vocabulary (new databank) required to prepare.

  • Daily two hours required to study newspapers and Editorials. (English)

  • Read at least two magazines, one novel each week.

  • Try for the shortcuts of mathematics. Calculations must be strong.

  • See any type of graph in any where you find, try to interpret that as fast as possible.

  • Change your attitude. Make one decision strictly. And only try for right decision or don't answer in case of ambiguity.

  • Read whatever you love. But try to read on different subjects articles.

  • Before three months of Cat, Daily solve one set of available question paper.

  • Try to make logical sense everywhere.

  • Try to solve calculations mentally.
