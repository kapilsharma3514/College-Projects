This year paper covered all the topics mentioned in GATE syllabus. There was no major changes in direction or pattern of exam as compared to previous year

In Mathematical Engineering sections some questions were from differential equations, matrices, mean value theorem, complex variables etc.

Some Observations from GATE 2016 EC Set 1 Paper -30th Jan, 2016

  • Set-1 paper wasy easy having formula based and conceptual based questions.
  • General Aptitude section is easy
  • All MCQ questions were easy and NTQ questions were bit tricky in calculation part.
  • Use of virtual calculator is simple for all calculations.
  • Excepted marks for topper is 80 marks
  • EMT and communication questions are more in the paper and easy to moderate questions are given from these subjects.
  • Questions were asked from Operation-amplifier, transmission lines, antennas, PCM, heterodyne receiver, Multiplexer, code conversion, basic circuit problems.
  • Analog questions were mixed with digital: Operation amplifier with counter (specific questions from analog was not asked)
  • Network questions were easy and some are formula based: Questions are given from Two port network, maximum power transfer theorem and circuit simplification of voltage and current based.
  • Mathematics Questions are asked from coordinate systems (surface integral), also given from fourier and laplace transforms and also there is one theory question from continuity and differentiability.
  • Many of 1 mark questions were MCQ type, and Many of 2 marks questions were of NTQ type.
  • Attempting General aptitude questions require no preparation, they were easy to answer.
  • Many of Technical questions are application based and needs thorough understanding of concepts in EMTL,ANALOG,EDC,Signals, etc.
  • Digital Circuits questions were easy.
  • Mathematics questions were moderate.
